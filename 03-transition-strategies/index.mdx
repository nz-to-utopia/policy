# Transition Strategies

<Callout type="draft">

</Callout>

## Overview

Knowing what's coming is only useful if we act on it. This section outlines specific strategies for helping industries, businesses, and workers adapt to AI-driven change.

The goal is not to resist change, but to manage it — ensuring that the benefits of AI are widely shared and that no one is left behind.
